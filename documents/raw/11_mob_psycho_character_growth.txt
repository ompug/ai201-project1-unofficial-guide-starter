Mob Psycho 100 is the recommendation for someone who wants action and comedy but also a kind story about self-control, insecurity, and becoming a better person. The surface pitch is about an extremely powerful psychic middle-schooler, but the emotional center is Mob learning that power does not solve identity or relationships.

MyAnimeList and IMDb both identify Mob Psycho 100 as a well-known supernatural action comedy. It fits the must-watch list because it combines wild animation with a surprisingly gentle message: growth matters more than dominance.

Recommendation note: use Mob Psycho 100 for viewers asking for creative animation, comedy with heart, supernatural fights, or a shorter modern series. It may not satisfy someone looking for grim realism or a very serious tone.
