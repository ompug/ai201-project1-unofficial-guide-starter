The safest gateway path is Spirited Away or Your Name for a movie, and Cowboy Bebop or Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood for a series. Spirited Away and Your Name are easy to recommend because they are complete films with broad appeal. Cowboy Bebop works for style and music. Brotherhood works when the viewer wants a full adventure story with a strong ending.

The darker or more demanding picks should be matched carefully. Akira is best for cyberpunk and animation history. Grave of the Fireflies is best for emotional war drama, but it needs a warning because it is extremely sad. Evangelion is essential for psychological mecha and analysis, but it can frustrate viewers who want a clean ending. Attack on Titan is gripping serial action, but it becomes violent and morally heavy.

If a user asks for recent acclaimed anime, Frieren is the best first answer in this corpus. If they ask for time travel, choose Steins;Gate. If they ask for a funny but heartfelt supernatural series, choose Mob Psycho 100.
