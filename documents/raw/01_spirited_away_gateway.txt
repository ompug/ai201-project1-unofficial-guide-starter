Title: Must-Watch Anime Notes - Spirited Away as a Gateway Film
Source: Rotten Tomatoes best anime movies guide https://editorial.rottentomatoes.com/guide/best-anime-movies/ ; BFI 50 key anime films https://www.bfi.org.uk/sight-and-sound/lists/50-key-anime-films
Date: 2026-06-10
Category: movie
Rating: 5

Spirited Away is one of the safest first recommendations for someone who wants to understand why anime is taken seriously as cinema. It is approachable because the story follows a child entering a strange spirit world, but it is also rich enough for older viewers because it layers anxiety, wonder, labor, greed, and growing up into the same journey.

Rotten Tomatoes places Spirited Away near the top of its anime movie guide and emphasizes its wide critical appeal. BFI includes it in a broader historical list of key anime films, which matters because BFI is not only measuring fan popularity; it is treating anime as film history.

Recommendation note: use Spirited Away when the user asks for a beautiful gateway anime, a family-friendly must-watch, or a film that works even for someone who does not usually watch anime. Do not recommend it as the best choice for viewers specifically asking for gritty sci-fi, sports, or long serialized action.
