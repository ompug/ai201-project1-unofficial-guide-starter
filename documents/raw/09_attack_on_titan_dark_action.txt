Title: Must-Watch Anime Notes - Attack on Titan for Dark Serial Action

Attack on Titan is the must-watch recommendation for viewers who want high-stakes serialized action, mystery, political escalation, and big plot turns. It begins with a simple survival hook about humanity fighting Titans, then expands into a much larger conflict about history, propaganda, revenge, and cycles of violence.

Crunchyroll reported that the Final Season had major MyAnimeList attention at launch, and IMDb's page gives the basic premise for new viewers. The important recommendation detail is that Attack on Titan is gripping but not light. It gets darker and more morally complicated as it goes.

Recommendation note: use Attack on Titan for viewers asking for intense action, cliffhangers, large-scale plot reveals, or modern mainstream anime. Avoid it for someone seeking comfort, low violence, or standalone episodes.
